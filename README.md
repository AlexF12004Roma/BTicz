## What is BTicz

BTicz lets you monitor and control your Domoticz home automation system directly
from your phone, tablet or desktop — wherever you are. It connects **directly**
to your own Domoticz installation over your local network or the internet, with
no cloud middleman, no account, and no registration.

- 📊 **Dashboard** — see all your devices at a glance, with real-time updates
- 💡 **Device control** — lights, switches, shutters and actuators
- 🌡 **Sensors** — temperature, humidity and other environmental sensors
- 🔔 **Alarm panel** — arm/disarm with a secure PIN
- 📈 **History** — browse the event log for any device
- ⭐ **Favourites** — quick access to your most-used devices, synced from Domoticz
- 🔒 **Secure connection** — HTTPS/SSL, with self-signed certificate support
- 🌍 **English & Italian**, with more languages planned

Currently supports **Domoticz** (2020.x or later). Home Assistant and openHAB
support are on the roadmap.

## Status

BTicz is in **open beta** (first release: v0.1). It's completely free right
now, precisely because it's still beta — think of it as early access while
bugs get fixed and the app gets feedback from real Domoticz users.

## About this repository

This repository **does not contain the app's source code** — BTicz is closed-source
software. It exists purely as an **issue tracker**: the place to report bugs,
request features, or ask questions about the app.

Found something broken, or have an idea for BTicz? **[Open an issue](../../issues)** —
that's exactly what this repo is for. Please include your app version, your
operating system, and a clear description of the problem; it helps a lot.

You can also reach out directly at **bticz@ballini.it**.
